Bio

Welcome! I am currently a Ph.D. student at PRG (Perception and Robotics Group), University of Maryland - College Park, supervised by Prof. Yiannis Aloimonos and Dr. Cornelia Fermuller. Formerly I was a visiting student in Fast Lab, Zhejiang University (ZJU), supervised by Prof. Fei Gao.

My research interests are perception and motion planning for mobile robots. The research I appreciate is those trying to solve practical problems with elegant solutions. I am always willing to explore exciting technologies in Robotics field and use what I learned to help others. My dream is to build a super cool robot that can help people improve their life!

If you are interested in my research or would like to have a talk, feel free to drop me an email.
